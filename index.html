Our Exclusive Products

Chocolate Truffle

For all the chocolate lovers out there. The truffle is prepared from a rich ganache of chocolate dark compound to provide it a thick and sweet texture to compliment the sponge of the cake. If chocolate is a sin, then its a necessary sin.

Caramel Butterscotch

A moist vanilla sponge cake layered with a mixture of whipped cream and Caramel Butterscotch. The sides of the cake are coated with this mixture to make this the ultimate Caramel Butterscotch indulgence.

Pineapple Cake

A typical Hawaiian cake made with chopped pineapple inside. The pulp of real pineapple leaves a freshness of taste which makes the occasion all the more wonderful. A pineapple is the result of many flowers whose fruitlets have joined around the core.

Red velvet

Red Velvet Cake is not just a chocolate cake with red food colouring added. This cake is softer than most, “velvet-like”, and the chocolate taste is actually quite mild. It’s more like a cross between a vanilla and chocolate cake with a verysubtle tang from buttermilk. And it is generously smothered in a fluffy cream cheese frosting.

Dairy Milk Cake

Who doesnt love Dairy Milk? A cake made for all the lovers of that milky chocolate which makes us go back in time reminiscing when chocolate meant dairy milk. No dark chocolate, no nuts, just dairy milk. Infused with crunchy chocolate chips.

Dark Chocolate Crunch Cake

The Belgium Dark Chocolate Cake is a decadent dessert masterpiece, featuring layers of moist chocolate sponge soaked with dark chocolate ganache. It is enrobed in a rich fudge icing that adds a luxurious depth of flavor, complementing the intense chocolate experience. The cake is finished with a dipped glaze that solidifies into a satisfyingly crunchy texture, offering a delightful contrast to the soft, indulgent layers beneath.
